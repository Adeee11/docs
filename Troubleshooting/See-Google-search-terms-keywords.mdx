---
title: "How to See Google Search Terms (Keywords)"
---

- **Google encrypts all search queries and keywords:** Analytics and other website tools cannot access this data directly.
- **You must use Google Search Console (GSC):**\
  Set up a free GSC account to view actual search queries bringing traffic to your site.
- **Future analytics integrations:**\
  Even with future features that integrate GSC, you will still need a GSC account to unlock this search keyword data.

In summary, to see Google search terms for your website, always connect and use Google Search Console.
