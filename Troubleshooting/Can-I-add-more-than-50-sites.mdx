---
title: "Adding More Than 50 Sites"
---

- **Default allowance:**\
  All plans allow up to 50 separate sites per account at no extra charge.
- **No strict cap:**\
  You can have more than 50 sites; simply contact support to request an increase.
- **How to proceed:**\
  Reach out to the analytics provider, and they will help set up the ability to track additional sites beyond the standard limit.
