title: "Discount Policy "
---

Floo Analytics maintains consistent pricing—no promo codes, sales events, or temporary reductions are ever applied. Subscriptions are designed to be fair by offering the same rate to every customer.

The exception is a built-in benefit for annual payments: selecting a yearly plan entitles all customers to a guaranteed 17% reduction compared to monthly rates, ensuring access to the best value without the need for special deals or codes.

## Sales and Discount Approach

Floo Analytics does not engage in temporary sales or price drops—for example, during Black Friday. Pricing remains steady throughout the year, with a focus on consistent value rather than fluctuations or time-limited promotions.

There is a single way to receive a discount on your first bill: use a referral link from the affiliate program. This gives both you and the referring member a \$10 credit to start, ensuring a fair incentive structure for everyone involved.
